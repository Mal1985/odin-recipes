Keylime Cheesecake

This is a recipe for a creamy, flavorful, keylime cheesecake with a gram cracker crust . Sure to be a favorite, with the perfect balance of sweet and tanginess.

Ingredients

  • Gram Crackers
  • Butter
  • Cream Cheese
  • White Sugar
  • Lime Zest
  • Corn Startch
  • Eggs
  • Lime Juice

Steps

  1. Combine graham cracker crumbs with butter. Press into the bottom and partially up the sides of a 9-inch springform pan; refrigerate while you prepare the filling.
  2. Preheat the oven to 300 dgrees F (150 degrees C). Fill a shallow pan half full of hot water and place on the lower rack to minimize cracking.
  3. To make the filling: Beat cream cheese, sugar, lime zest, and cornstarch in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time, blending just until smooth. Add keylime juice with mixer on low. Finish mixing by hand. Do not over beat, or cake will crack during baking. Pour batter into the prepared crust.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 55 to 65 minutes, or until set.
  5. Turn the oven off. and let cheesecake stand in the oven for 30 minutes with the door open at least 4 inches. Remove from oven.
  6. Refrigerate cheesecake 8 hours to over night, and up to 3 days.
